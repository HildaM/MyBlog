Spring is the most beautiful season in the country. All kinds of flowers are in bud, like shy little girls; the grass comes out of the soil and looks at the colorful world curiously;
the trees take out green branches; the farmer drives the total cattle in the field, followed by many starlings and egrets, some of them are flying low,

The charming countryside is full of colorful, pure and beautiful flowers.
There are peony, Phoenix, cockscomb and Dahlia. They are simple and gorgeous, showing a unique rural scenery.
The sun sprinkles the seeds like gold. The sun bathes the flowers, like a layer of golden gauze, and stands in front of the door.

In summer, the sun scorches the earth. If there is a small river behind their house,
they will meet a group of children playing in the water by the stone bridge and under the shade of green trees.
From time to time, they will splash layers of water like a blooming white lotus. No one knows what happened between the colorful streams and the stones.
